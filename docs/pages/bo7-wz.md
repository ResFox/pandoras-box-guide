## Setup checklist

- Download [DControl](https://example.com/dcontrol). WinRAR password: **sordum**. Run `DControl.exe` and disable Windows Defender through it.
- Install [Visual C++ all-in-one](https://www.techpowerup.com/download/visual-c-redistributable-runtime-package-all-in-one/) and [DirectX](https://download.microsoft.com/download/1/7/1/1718ccc4-6315-4d8e-9543-8e28a4e18c4c/dxwebsetup.exe).
- Remove third-party antivirus.
- Game: **borderless windowed**.
- Windows display scale: **100%**.
- **Secure Boot** + **HVCI** on.

## Avoiding bans & shadowbans

These are practical notes from user reports and long-term use.

### Shadowban — what it actually is

You can still access the game, but matchmaking gets routed to a bad pool:
high ping, long queues, and flagged/sweaty lobbies.

### Account shielding

!!! info
    Works best if you build stats slowly and keep behaviour realistic.

#### What it means

You're shaping account progression so it doesn't look impossible.

#### BIOS / Windows checklist

1. TPM 2.0 on  
2. Virtualization on (VT-x / SVM)  
3. Secure Boot on  
4. Search **Core Isolation** → **Memory Integrity** → ON → reboot  

#### Stat hygiene (main account)

### First week

- Pick **one thing**: walls **or** aim — not both.
- Keeps your curve from spiking overnight.

### After that

- Add the second feature slowly. Aim = soft at first. Walls = **low render distance** so you're not tracing people through terrain.

### If you're grinding stats

- ~**30k kills per mode**, legit or with **only one** cheat feature at a time.
- **Public only** — **not ranked**.
- Goal is believable progression, not a vertical line on the chart.

#### Accounts to avoid

- Already shadowbanned.
- Shadowbanned within the last ~**3 months** (easier to re-flag — "blacklist" loop).

#### Hardware / behaviour

- No Cronus-style spoofers.
- Don't trace people through walls.
- Don't spray through geometry.

!!! danger
    Don't run **full headshot** mode.

### Playtime & K/D

#### Hours

- Idle in matchmaking to pad time — that's fine.

#### What not to do

- AFK bots — risky.

#### Safer AFK

- Casual, one bot kill, walk away and let deaths / respawns stack.

#### K/D

- Let it bleed down while you're doing something else with the game open, or play another title.

#### Avoid

- Bot-lobby tools  
- Unlock-all services  

#### Past hardware ban

- New motherboard is the clean slate option people actually use.

### Precision

You're probably not a LAN kid — don't hold **pro-tier** accuracy stats.

Farm casual until your averages look human.

### Ranked — account level

Don't queue ranked on a fresh account. Use **level 110+** mains.
